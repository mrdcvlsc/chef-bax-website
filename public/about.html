History

The business was established in May 2020 during the height of the pandemic where lockdowns were imposed in many places in the Philippines.


That time we overproduced baked "Banana Loaf"s so we decided to sell it. We advertised it on our subdivision's facebook page, and there's a lot of people in our neighborhood who liked it.

Service

A custom cake is any type of cake that goes above and beyond the call of regular cake work. This can include handmade fondant pieces, flowers, or attention to detail that is a little bit extra than a traditional style birthday cake. Tiered cakes and wedding cakes are also considered custom.
